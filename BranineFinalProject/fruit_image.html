Best Fruit Salad for Summer

Fruit Salad is a mixture of different types of sweet fruit served in juice or syrup. It's healthy to make you satiated in summer time. to learn how to prepare it follow the steps:

    >Get the Ingredients
    * Nuts: 3/4 cup Almonds or 1/4 cup Pecans (preferred)
    * 1/4 cup Rose water (preferred)
    * 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice + 1/2teaspoon grated lemon zest
    * 1 cups cubed fresh pineapple
    * 1 cups strawberries, hulled and sliced
    * 2 kiwi fruit, peeled and sliced
    * 2 bananas, sliced
    * 1 oranges, peeled and sectioned
    * 1/2 cup seedless grapes
    * 1 cups blueberries

    >Step 1 Place all the fruits you cut and prepared in a medium bowl.

    >Step 2 Bring your Rose water, lemon juice, 2 teaspoons of sugar, and lemon zest to your boil in a saucepan over medium heat. and simmer until slightly thickened. After 2 minutes: Remove from heat, and set aside to cool.

    >Step 3 Bring the bowl that include the fruits. Pour the cooled sauce over the fruit. Cover and refrigerate for 1h or 2h before serving.

This serving is made for 2 persons. You can add any fruits you like. This fruit salad is really easy to prepare. You can double or triple the recipe for parties and holidays.
